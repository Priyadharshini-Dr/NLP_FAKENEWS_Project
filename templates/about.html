About Us

This project was developed to detect fake news using Natural Language Processing (NLP). It analyzes headlines and article text to determine if a news piece is real or fake.

Our mission is to combat misinformation by providing a quick and reliable AI-powered detection tool. We believe in promoting truth, transparency, and critical thinking in the digital world.
